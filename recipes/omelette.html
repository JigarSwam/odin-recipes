Omelette

A warm, fluffy omelette to start your day on the right foot.

In less than 5 minutes you can create a delicious breakfast to charge you for the day

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese
  • 3 Mushrooms
  • 1/2 White Onion
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Dice onion
  2. Slice mushrooms
  3. Crack eggs into a bowl
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Whisk
  6. Warm pan on medium heat
  7. Add butter to pan
  8. Spread melted butter around pan
  9. Add whisked eggs and spread around pan
  10. Add onions and mushrooms
  11. As parts of the omelette begin to solidify, move it to the edges by using your spatula and allow for outer liquid to come to center
  12. Change heat to medium-low or low
  13. Add cheese to one half and fold omelette over
  14. Serve
