Grilled Cheese

A warm, not to crunchy grilled cheese.

In less than 10 minutes you can have a filling lunchtime meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Sliced cheese of your choice
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Warm pan on medium heat and drop some butter on the pan until it melts
  2. Spread butter on both sides of your bread
  3. Once butter on the pan is bubbling, place slices of bread on pan
  4. After 1-3 minutes flip bread
  5. Add slices of cheese on both sides
  6. After 1-2 minutes combine slices of bread
  7. Change heat to low and wait for cheese to melt
  8. Remove from heat and let cool for 30 seconds
  9. Slice sandwich in half and serve
