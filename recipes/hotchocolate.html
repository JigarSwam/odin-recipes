Hot Chocolate

A hot beverage to keep you warm on a cold night (or any night).

Enjoy a cup of delicious hot chocolate while you snuggle under the blanket and watch your favorite movie.

Ingredients

  • Hot Chocolate Powder
  • Whole Milk
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Measure one cup of whole milk into a pot
  2. Heat on medium-high
  3. Once milk is hot (not boiling) add hot chocolate powder and stir
  4. After incorporated, lower heat to medium and continue to let heat up
  5. Pour hot chocolate into mug
  6. Top with whipped cream
